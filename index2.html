About Me

I am a dedicated and enthusiastic piano teacher with a Master degree in Music Education and Piano Performance and over fifteen years of piano teaching experience. My piano studio offers private piano lessons to students of all ages/abilities in Palo Alto, California. Under my teaching, students experience firsthand that music is a creative art as well as an interpretive art. They learn not only learn how to express themselves through artistic performances of various styles of music, but they also learn how to make their own music through improvisation and composition. If you are looking for top-quality musical instruction that will give the student a life-long understand and appreciation of music, you will find it here! I’ve been offering comprehensive and affordable private lessons in Palo Alto since 2015. My job is a passion for me, and it brings me joy to be able to share this passion with my students. From day one, they learn important foundational techniques to help them boost their skills and self-esteem. Each one has a unique learning style, and it’s my duty to find out what that is and adapt my lessons accordingly. Next

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is your lesson?

Lessons will be held once a week for 30 mins, 45 mins and 60 mins. Students should arrive on time and prepared with all music books needed for the lesson.

Do you have specific teaching qualifications?

I have a Master degree in Music Education from Penn State University, specialized in early childhood music education. I have a bachelor degree in piano performance from National Taiwan Normal University. I taught in elementary and middle school class before as a music teacher.

Do you make up for lesson?

When you register for lessons, your timeslot is reserved for you for the following quarter. In the event that student has to miss a lesson because of an emergency or illness, please contact me with as much advance notice as possible. Then a make-up lesson will be scheduled at the earliest possible date.

What kind of piano is suitable?

Acoustic piano is the best, either upright or grand. Some of my students also use digital piano.

Do you have group class or studio recital?

Yes, and it is an excited time for students! There will be a recital and studio group lesson offer every school year. Students are encouraged to participate to demonstrate their learning progress and to share their music with friends and family members.

Please contact me if you have any questions.
